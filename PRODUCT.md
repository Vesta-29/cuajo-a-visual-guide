# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Beginners looking to learn the heritage Filipino card game of Cuajo.

## Product Purpose
To break down the complex rules of Cuajo into easily digestible, highly visual, and interactive sections.

## Brand Personality
Premium, Educational, Heritage, Visually rich.

## Anti-references
Dense unformatted text walls, generic Wikipedia-style articles, bland corporate styles.

## Design Principles
- Show, don't tell: Use interactive scenarios and dynamic highlighting over paragraphs of text.
- Accessible comfort: Support multiple reading modes (classic, faded, night) for legibility.
- Heritage pride: Celebrate the unique history and roots of the game.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Responsive across mobile and desktop. Support multiple contrast themes (day/night/faded).
